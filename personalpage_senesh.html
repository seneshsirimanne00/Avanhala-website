Hoppers(Appa)

Hoppers , aka the notable "appa" is one of the most popular and lovable food found in sri lanka.plain hoppers are a typical bowl shaped pancake whitch is made from fermented rice flour and coconut milk.they are made in special bowl shaped pans. they come out of the pan with crispy round edgess and athick smooth middle at the bottom.simply enjoyed by all ages a simple hopper is a food that can be eaten at any time.






Our favorite food the hoppers have evolved into many forms , most notabaly the egg hopper ,a plain hopper with a an egg in the middle , simple but tastes a lot better ,mostly enjoyed in evenings.
Filled hoppers can be found in ella ,this amazing form of hoppers.
curd and tricle hoppers or as they say in sinhela "pani appa " is a hopper that is most favored by children because of it's undeniable sweetness,also we can't forget string hoppers, string hoppers are also one of the most commonly eaten hoppers in sri lanka.
